Steak


Description

These juicy steaks are the perfect way to start your day. They are easy to prepare and perfect for a family gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander

Steps

  1. Step 1

    In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, and coriander.

  2. Step 2

    Remove the steak from the skillet and let rest for 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Slice the steak.

  4. Step 4

    Serve the steak with your favorite side dishes.
