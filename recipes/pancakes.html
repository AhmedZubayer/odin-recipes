Pancakes


Description

These fluffy pancakes are a great breakfast treat. They are easy to make and perfect for a family gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Step 1

    In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.

  2. Step 2

    In a separate bowl, whisk together the melted butter, milk, buttermilk, and sugar.

  3. Step 3

    Stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir in the eggs, one at a time, then use a spatula to gently fold the eggs into the batter.

  4. Step 4

    Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip the pancakes and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes more. Repeat with the remaining batter.
